Market Segmentation
The process of dividing a broad consumer or business market into sub-groups based on shared characteristics.
Target Market
A specific group of consumers at which a company aims its products and services.
Market Research
The process of gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information about a market, including information about the target market and customers.

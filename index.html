About Us

Hi there, we’re Sid, James, Sai, and Aryan. We’re teenagers passionate about coding and DECA. Starting in a small classroom, we found challenges with the DECA learning process. We experienced first hand the pressure and excitement that came with preparing for conferences. There was a need for a centralized platform where DECA students could find high-quality resources, share knowledge, and connect with like-minded peersWith a shared goal, we founded DECAlingo, a platform that helps fellow DECA members with their learning processes.

DECAlingo is a beta platform that offers lesson plans, vocabulary quizzes, and practice tests that elevate students' knowledge and efficiency with the different topics. We aim to guide students in discovering their specialty whether it’s Marketing, Hospitality, Finance, or Entrepreneurship!

We’re still adapting the user experience and the layout. However, we hope you join us in this journey to both excel at DECA and experience high quality learning. Thank you for being part of our community, and we look forward to supporting you on your path to success.
